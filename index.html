My Journey

Original Interest

I first grew an interest in the computer science field as I watched my dad, who was a former developer at Samsung, work with computers and software.

Scratch And Unity

In my elementary school years, I started with MIT's kid-friendly coding website, Scratch. I spent hours on tutorials, trying to build something memorable. When I got used to Scratch's block coding system, I wanted to progress into what I considered "real" coding. Since I was still hooked on game development, I heard of and started working on a project in Unity's game engine. It was a steep learning curve, but it taught me that I needed a real foundation in coding, and above all, a drive to keep going.

Recent Years

So recently, I've been familiarizing myself with the coding language Java. As a current high school student with AP Computer Science experience, I've set my sights on building an app that can revolutionize the ease of access of various food cultures, starting with my own.
