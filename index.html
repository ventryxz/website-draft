My Journey

The Roots

I know computer science is a common major, but for me, it’s more than a desk job. Growing up with a father who was a software developer in South Korea, I naturally gravitated toward computers. My passion started exactly where many great journeys do: with games.

Trial & Error

At ten, I started with Scratch. I spent hours on tutorials, trying to build something memorable. When I outgrew block coding, I jumped into Unity. It was a steep learning curve—honestly, a bit too steep at the time—but it taught me that I needed a real foundation in logic before I could build the worlds I imagined.

The Mission

That realization led me to Java. From Udemy to AP Computer Science A, I’ve moved from "playing" with code to "building" with it. My ultimate goal? To build an app that shares my culture with the world through the universal language of food.

"I may not be a master developer yet, but I have a stubborn dream to create something that benefits people's daily lives."
